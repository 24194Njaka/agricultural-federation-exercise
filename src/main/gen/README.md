# Business Rules
- A collectivity must have a unique number and unique name assigned by the federation
- Once assigned, the number and name cannot be changed
- A collectivity needs at least 10 members with 5 having 6+ months seniority
- A new member needs at least 2 confirmed member sponsors with 90+ days seniority
- Sponsors from target collectivity must be >= sponsors from other collectivities
- A collectivity can have only one CASH account
- Bank account number must have 23 digits (5+5+11+2)
